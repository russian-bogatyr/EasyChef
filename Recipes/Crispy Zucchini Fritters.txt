Crispy Zucchini Fritters

Time
30 minutes

Ingredients
3 1/3 cups grated zucchini (courgette)
6 spring onions, chopped
1 bunch fresh dil, chopped
Salt to taste
1 egg, beaten
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1 clove garlic, minced
2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Instructions
1. Combine zucchini, spring onions, and dil in a large bowl. Stir in salt. Add egg; stir until well-combined. Stir in flour and garlic until batter comes together.
2. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Drop tablespoonfuls of batter into hot oil. Fry until browned and cooked through, about 3 minutes per side.
