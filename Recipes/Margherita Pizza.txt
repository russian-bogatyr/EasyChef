Margherita Pizza

Time
125 minutes

Ingredients
1/2 cup crushed tomatoes
oregano
1 1/2 salt
pepper
3 tablespoons olive oil
1/2 pound diced mozzarella
basil
3 3/4 cups flour
1 1/3 cup warm water
1 tablespoon sugar
1 packet yeast

Instructions
Pizza Dough:
1. Whisk flour and salt. Make a well and add warm water, sugar, and yeast. When foamy, mix in olive oil; knead until smooth, 5 minutes.
2. Brush with olive oil, cover in a bowl and let rise until doubled, about 1 hour 30 minues. Divide into two 1 poud balls. Use 1 pound per recipe.
Pizza:
1. Stretch dough into two thin9-inch rounds. Top each with crushed tomatoes, oregano, salt, pepper, and olive oil; bake until golden at 500 degrees Farenheit for 15 minutes, until cheese melts, the drizzle olive oil.
