Penne alla Vodka

Time
25 minutes

Ingredients
1 pound penne pasta
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 whole medium onion, chopped finely
3 tablespoons butter
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 cup vodka
One 14-ounce can tomato sauce
1 cup heavy cream
1 pinch red pepper flakes
1/4 to 1/2 teaspoon salt
Freshly ground black pepper
Grated parmesan

Instructions
1. Saute the garlic and onion in 2 tablespoons butter and the olive oil in a saucepan over medium heat until soft. Pour in the vodka. Cook and reduce for 2 to 3 minutes, then pour in the tomato sauce.
2. Stir in the mixture until it is thoroughly, then reduce the heat to low. Pour in the heavy cream. Stir to combine, then turn heat to the lowest simmer possible. Sprinkle in the red pepper flakes and add slat and pepper to taste.
3. Finally, stir in the remaining 1 tablespoon butter. Pour the drained pasta into the sauce and toss to combine. Sprinkle on the parmesan, then sprinkle on more red pepper flakes if desired.
