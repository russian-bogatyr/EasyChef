Chinese Beef and Broccoli

Time
20 minutes

Ingredients
1 pound lean beef
1 tablespoon soy sauce
1 tablespoon sherry wine
2 tablespoons oil
1 small yellow onion
1 tablespoon minced fresh garlic
1 tablespoon minced fresh ginger
4 cups broccoli
1 cup bean sprouts

Sauce Ingredients
1/3 cup soy sauce
1/3 cup water
1/3 cup brown sugar
1 tablespoon sherry wine
1 tablespoon toasted sesame seed oil
1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
1 1/2 tablespoon cornstarch

Instructions
1. Toss the beef strips with the soy sauce and sherry wine. Set aside until ready to use.
2. Combine the sauce ingredietns until the cornstarch is dissolved. Set aside until ready to use.
3. Heat the oil over high heat in a wok or large heavy skillet. Once, very hot, add the beef and fry until lightly browned, about 2 minutes. Add the onions, garlic, and ginger and fry for another minute. Add a little more oil if necessary. Add the broccoli and fry for another minute or until crisp-tender.
4. Stir in the bean sprouts. Stir in the sauce and simmer for another minute.
5. Serve immediately with steamed rice.
