Chinese Chicken Egg Fried Rice

Time
15 minutes

Ingredients
2 cups cooked long grain rice
2 eggs
1 cup chopped frozen vegetables
1 chicken breast
3 spring onions
2 garlic cloves
2 cm fresh root ginger
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
2 tablespoons soy sauce
1 tablespoon oyster sauce
1 teaspoon sesame oil
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley

Instructions
1. Heat up the wok, cut the chicken into small pieces and season with salt, pepper, and garlic powder. Add on tablespoon of oil to the wok and stir fry on high heat until the chicken is cooked through and slightly browned.
2. Remove from the wok and set aside.
3. Chop the spring onions, garlic, and ginger, add to the wok with another tablespoon of oil and stir fry for 30 seconds.
4. Beat the eggs slightly, add them to the wok and scramble them, then add the cooked rice, vegetables, chicken, sesame oil, soy sauce, oyster sauce, and mix well to combine.
5. Stir fry for a further 3 mintues, then add the chopped parsley and remove from the heat.
6. Serve immediately.
